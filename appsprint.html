AppSprint 2020

Hello developers and designers!

DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES TO UNDERSTAND AND BUILD MOBILE APPLICATIONS?


Come ahead and join US as we take you through this exciting week of UI design and development! We will be taking you through the entire process of building an app right from the core principles of designing an app to coding it into a functional application you can use in the real world. With webinars aimed at taking you through the fundamentals, you can mark your start at making your ideas into real-life applications. After the webinars, there will be an intensely competitive yet beginner-friendly set of events aimed at testing out what you learnt in the community. 

Join us on Devpost Here

The Schedule


  • Webinar One

                 

    UI Design by Akash Hamirwasia

    Learn how to make a design and turn your ideas into reality with this action packed session on Figma by Akash. Tune in on 25th July between 3:30 PM to 5 PM.

  • Webinar Two

    App Development by Irfan Syed

    Learn how to turn your design into a functional mobile application with a tutorial of Android Development by Irfan. Tune on 25th July between 3:30 PM and 5 PM

  • UI Design Challenge

    Subtitle

    The first stage will be a design challenge where you will be expected to make the design and prototype your idea into reality by making fun micro-interactions and beautiful and aesthetic designs. Nowadays every tech company is pushing forward to make their apps the most crowd-pleasing in the market. Every great app starts with great design. Don't worry if you have absolutely no idea about how to get started, the webinars will definitely get you started on how all of this shall work.

  • App Design Challenge

    Subtitle

    The next stage will engage you in making your design a perfectly functional mobile application. Code your app into existence using the language of your choice, let it be Java, Kotlin, Swift, Flutter or React native.
